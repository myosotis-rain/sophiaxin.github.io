The Challenge

Students and young professionals working independently in mobile environments struggle to balance productivity, stress, and community connection. Digital apps provide limited emotional engagement and fail to create meaningful social bonds.

Our Innovation

A portable keychain interface that provides tangible, physical feedback through a shape-changing plant representation. The device intelligently tracks well-being and creates community through proximity-based interactions and collectible decorations.

How It Works

Grows & Wilts

Plant grows when you're productive, wilts when stressed - providing immediate visual feedback

Community Lights

Devices in vicinity light up, creating awareness of other TUI users nearby

Collectible Connection

Clip-on decorations help build community through shared collectibles
